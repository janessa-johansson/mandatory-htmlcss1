Established in 2001, Fairway Greenhouse began as a hobby, then grew into a business due to the support of our lovely community! Today, we house a number of beautiful plants and grow them year round. One of our specialties is hawkflower, a hardy perennial with big, gorgeous pink blooms. It's easy to take care of and looks lovely in offices, waiting rooms, and homes alike.

Our greenhouse is open year round. Come by any time for a tour, to meet our plants, or to just have a fika!
